Privacy Policy

Last Update: March 24, 2025

At Véon, we offer secure and flexible payment options to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit & Debit Cards – Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover
  • PayPal – Secure and fast checkout with your PayPal account
  • Apple Pay & Google Pay – Quick payments using your mobile wallet
  • Shop Pay – Fast and secure checkout with saved details
  • Klarna & Afterpay (if applicable) – Buy now, pay later with flexible installment options

2. Secure Transactions

Your security is our priority. All payments are processed through encrypted and trusted payment gateways to keep your information safe.

3. Currency & International Payments

We accept multiple currencies, and international transactions may be subject to conversion rates. Your bank or provider may apply additional fees.

4. Need Help?

If you have any payment-related questions or issues, feel free to contact our support team. We're here to assist you!

5. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, including:

  • Items marked as final sale
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Intimate or undergarment wear for hygiene reasons

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, contact us immediately. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

7. Need Assistance?

If you have any questions about our return and refund process, our support team is happy to help. Reach out through our Contact Us page, and we’ll assist you as soon as possible.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our website implies acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our support team. We’re here to assist you!
